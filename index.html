Inter-University Hackathon

Organized by
Industrial Management Science Students' Association
University of Kelaniya

hackX, the most awaited inter-university hackathon in Sri Lanka, organized by the Industrial Management Science Students' Association will unfold its third chapter on the 21st and 22nd of September and once again, provide the undergraduates of this great country the opportunity to "Make Ideas Happen".

hackX 2k18 is open to all undergraduates; 15 teams will be selected as finalists after a thorough evaluation of the applications submitted. The finalists will compete in a 12-hour long coding endeavor, during which they will design, develop, implement and commercialize their novel ideas. The finalists will subsequently pitch their ideas to an expert panel of judges together with a prototype demonstration, through which the winners will be chosen.
